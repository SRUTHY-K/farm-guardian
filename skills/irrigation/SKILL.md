# Irrigation Advisor Skill

Use this skill when advising on watering:
1. Recommend water requirements based on crop stage (e.g., flowering tomato needs uniform moisture, rice needs standing water in early stages).
2. Adjust irrigation plans for recent and forecasted rain (e.g., skip watering if heavy rain is forecasted within 24 hours).
3. Advise on water-efficient methods (drip, sprinkler) to conserve water resources.
