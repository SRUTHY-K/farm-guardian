# Government Schemes & Subsidies Skill

Use this skill when explaining subsidies or assistance:
1. Provide summaries of standard agricultural programs (e.g., PM-KISAN, crop insurance schemes, organic farming subsidies, solar pump subsidies).
2. Check eligibility criteria: landholding limits, crop type, location.
3. Walk the farmer through required documents (Aadhaar, land records, bank details).
4. **Important**: Request human confirmation before drafting application forms or documents on behalf of the farmer.
