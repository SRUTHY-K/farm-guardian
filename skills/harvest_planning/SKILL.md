# Harvest Planning & Post-Harvest Skill

Use this skill when farmers ask about maturity and harvesting:
1. Estimate maturity dates using typical days-to-harvest from planting date.
2. Highlight indicators of harvest readiness (e.g., tomato color, paddy grain hardness, moisture levels).
3. Offer best practices for post-harvest handling (sorting, packaging, cold storage) to minimize crop losses and maintain quality.
