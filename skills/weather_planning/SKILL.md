# Weather Risk & Advisory Skill

Use this skill when interpreting weather forecasts for farmers:
1. Examine temperature, humidity, wind, and rain projections.
2. Check for alerts:
   - **Rain alerts**: If rain is expected, advise against spraying fertilizers or pesticides.
   - **Heat stress warnings**: Suggest early morning/evening watering and mulching.
   - **Frost warnings**: Recommend covering vulnerable crops or light evening irrigation.
3. Suggest preventative actions based on weather hazards to build climate resilience.
