# Market Prices & Selling Strategy Skill

Use this skill when farmers ask about selling their crops:
1. Provide latest wholesale market (Mandi) price range for the specified crop in nearby locations.
2. Recommend the best days to sell based on typical price patterns and local demand.
3. Suggest transport and storage tips (e.g., drying grains, cooling vegetables) to fetch better prices.
4. Estimate profit potential based on yield and price trends.
